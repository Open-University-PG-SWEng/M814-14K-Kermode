REQ-ID: WS_NFR001
Description: The system shall allow to rearrange an appointment in an efficient way.
Type: Performance
Rationale: Rearranging appointments often is done during a phone call, hence the receptionist should be able to carry out this request fast.
Fit Criterion: The receptionist should be able to rearrange an appointment in less than one minute.
